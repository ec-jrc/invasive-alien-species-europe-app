Invasive alien species (IAS) can have severe effects to biodiversity, ecosystems and human activities. Be a part of the effort to confront the worst IAS in Europe! This application enables you to report IAS occurrences in your area, and thus you contribute to early detections of new invaders.

The procedure to provide feedback on the observations received from the public is still under development and will become available in subsequent releases of the app.
